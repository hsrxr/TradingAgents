Title: Bitcoin stalls below key resistance as analysts clash over next move
Source: CoinDesk
URL: https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/04/09/bitcoin-stalls-below-key-resistance-as-analysts-clash-over-next-move

The crypto market remains pinned just below its early-February ceiling, with bitcoinBTC$71,324.31hovering at $71,200 and ether (ETH) trading at $2,185. The sideways crawl comes despite a risk-on boost from the recent US-Iran ceasefire, leaving analysts sharply divided on the next leg.

Bloomberg's Mike McGlone said this week that BTCneeds to reclaim $75,000or risk a meltdown to $10,000. Fundstrat Tom Lee has taken a contrasting view, claiming that the"bottom is in"on Wednesday, although it's worth noting that his fund holds $10.4 billion worth of ETH.

BTC is up by around 0.3% since midnight UTC while ETH is flat having outperformed the broader market on Wednesday, and while BTC has posted a modest gain, all eyes remain on whether this range-bound stability is a launchpad or a trap.
